Maggi

Description

Maggi is most loved noodles in India. Koyi bhi mana nahi karne wali khana maggi hi hai.

Ingredients

  • Maggi noodles - 1packet
  • Water - 150ml
  • Oil -1ts
  • Onion - half cup
  • Chilli Powder - 1ts
  • Turmeric - 1/2ts
  • Salt - 1/2ts
  • Coriender Powder - 1/2ts

Steps

  1. First add oil along with onions and cook until onions turn into pink.
  2. Now add water and also add noodles.
  3. Add maggi masala, chilli powder, turmeric,salt and coriender powder.
  4. Cook until water disappers and noodles becomes soft.
  5. Simple and tasty maggi noodles is ready!
