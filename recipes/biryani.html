Biryani

Description

Biryani is the most loved recipe in the world. It is easy to make but ingredients have to be blended in right proportion to have finger-licking biryani.

Ingredients

  • Chicken-500gm
  • Rice - 500gm
  • Chilli powder - ar
  • Turmeric - 1ts
  • Salt -ar
  • All mix spices
  • Garam masala - 2ts
  • Curd
  • Lemons - 1nos
  • Fried onions
  • Ghee - 2ts
  • Oil - 400ml
  • Milk - 1cup

Steps

  1. Wash the meat with water and turmeric.
  2. Add chilli powder-3ts, turmeric-1ts, salt -1ts, all mix spices and garam masala-1ts./li>
  3. Mix the ingredients well and add curd, fried onions and lemon.
  4. Marinate for 3hrs(Whole night is preffered).
  5. Meanwhile boil the rice in water with few spices and salt till 80% of rice is cooked.
  6. Take briyani bowl, put marinated chicken at the bottom and add 1ts ghee.
  7. Add the boiled rice on the chicken layer.
  8. On rice layer add ghee, milk and few more fried onions.
  9. Now seal the bowl with a lid with a little opening.
  10. Cook it on high flame for 10min and low flame for 10min.
  11. Keep it aside for 15min and serve on ur plates.
  12. Your delicious biryani is ready!
